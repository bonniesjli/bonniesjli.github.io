About Me

Hello! I'M Bonnie

I'm a 18 year old AI researcher, striving to develop the future of intelligence.

I'm an undergrad at McGill University in Mathematics and Computer Science. I'm working at Mila on model-based reinforcement learning with Joelle Pineau.

At 16, I worked on reinforcement learning at Mila in Yoshua Bengio's group. More recently, I worked on imitation learning at Vector Institute with Bradly Stadie and Jimmy Ba. I'm interested in meta learning, hierachical RL, and imitation learning. Shoot me an email if you want to get in touch!
